# ABOUT_COGNOS
Cognos Formação e Desenvolvimento Pessoal is a certified Portuguese vocational training institution, adult education provider and online training organization, operating since 2009. We specialize in e-learning and professional development for adults in Portugal and Portuguese-speaking markets, offering more than 200 programs across Management, Health, Psychology, Criminology, Education, Gerontology, Languages and Occupational Safety.

Our training portfolio includes:
- Continuing Education Courses (short, skill-focused programs)
- Advanced Training Courses (technical programs for learners with secondary education)
- Specialization Programs (advanced qualifications for graduates)

All programs are delivered fully online through a flexible e-learning model, with self-paced study, dedicated trainer support and SIGO-certified completion.

# E_LEARNING_MODEL
description:
  - 100% e-learning courses (no physical presence required)
  - No pre-defined study schedule; the student studies at their own pace
  - Materials provided on the platform (manuals, videos, presentations, scientific articles, practical work)
  - Direct contact with trainers through asynchronous (messages) and synchronous (chat, video, audio) sessions
  - Assessments conducted from home, within the course period
  - Final certification issued via the SIGO platform, valid for the 40 hours of mandatory continuing education
