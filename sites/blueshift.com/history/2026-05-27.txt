# Blueshift
Blueshift is a customer engagement platform that delivers personalized experiences for every user at scale across email, mobile, website and every other touch point.

Key terms: Customer engagement platform, cross channel marketing platform, email marketing, mobile marketing, customer data platform, email personalization, multi channel messaging platform

## Website Description
Blueshift empowers brands to drive customer-centric engagement by transforming data into actionable intelligence across every marketing channel. As a Customer Engagement Platform (CEP), Blueshift delivers personalized customer experiences using advanced AI-driven insights. Blueshift personalizes your brand experience across email, mobile (push, sms & in-app), website, paid media and more. 


What Does Blueshift Do?
- **Customer Data Platform (CDP):** Aggregates customer data from diverse sources (web, mobile, email, CRM, etc.) into unified profiles.
- **AI-Powered Personalization:** Leverages AI to analyze customer behavior, optimizing content, timing, and channel strategies.
- **Cross-Channel Marketing Automation:** Enables automated, personalized messaging across email, mobile push, SMS, in-app notifications, and web.
- **Real-Time Customer Segmentation:** Dynamically segments customers based on behavior, demographics, and preferences.
- **Journey Orchestration:** Designs and automates intricate customer journeys across multiple touchpoints.
- **Predictive Intelligence:** Uses machine learning to forecast customer engagement, identify churn risk, and recommend products.
- **Integrations & APIs:** Seamlessly connects with third-party tools (e.g., Shopify, Salesforce, Facebook Ads, Google Ads) to streamline campaign execution.

Who Uses Blueshift?
- **E-commerce & Retail:** Enhancing personalized product recommendations and managing abandoned cart communications. Blueshift powers personalization for brands like Sweetwater.
- **Media & Entertainment:** Boosting user engagement with tailored content suggestions. Blueshift powers brands like BBC & Discovery.
- **Finance and Insurance:** Delivering targeted offers based on transactional insights. Blueshift works with brands like LendingTree.
- **Credit unions: **Cross-sell products to members. Blueshift works with brands like Suncoast Credit Union.
- **eLearning: **Promote courses and premium content. Blueshift works with leading eLearning sites like Udacity.com
- **Healthcare: **Educate your users and promote content. Blueshift is HIPAA compliant and works clients like GoHealth.
