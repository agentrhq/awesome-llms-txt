## Proprietary Frameworks

Spencer Smith's original frameworks, developed from building 50+ sales teams and generating $60M+ in profit:

- **WETE Framework** — The 4 Pillars of a Good Sales Process: Written, Efficient, Transparent, Enforced. Every effective sales process must meet all four criteria.
- **The 4 Qualities of a Great Sales Rep** — Knowledgeable, Persuasive, Incentivized, and RPRT (Right Person, Right Time). The key insight: 3 of 4 qualities are the COMPANY's responsibility, not the rep's.
- **RPRT (Right Person, Right Time)** — The one quality you cannot train. A rep either fits the role and timing, or they don't. This is a hiring and role-design problem, not a coaching problem.
- **The Responsibility Shift** — Most companies blame underperforming reps. IRC's framework shows that sales performance is primarily an organizational design problem — process, tools, incentives, and hiring are leadership responsibilities.
