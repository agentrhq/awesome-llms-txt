## About Sardine

Sardine is an AI-powered fraud prevention, compliance, and risk management platform serving banks, fintechs, credit unions, and financial institutions worldwide. Sardine provides end-to-end solutions spanning identity verification, device intelligence, behavioral biometrics, transaction monitoring, and case management to stop fraud before it happens.

**Mission:** Protect financial institutions and their customers from fraud using AI-driven intelligence and real-time risk scoring.

**Contact:** https://www.sardine.ai/demo

## Products

- KYC (Know Your Customer) - Identity verification and customer onboarding solutions
- KYB (Know Your Business) - Business verification and due diligence
- Device & Behavior Intelligence - Device fingerprinting and behavioral biometrics for fraud detection
- AI Agents - AI-powered fraud and compliance automation tools
- Account Takeover Prevention - Real-time detection and prevention of account takeover attacks
- Bot Detection - Identify and block automated bot activity
- Transaction Monitoring - AML/BSA compliance and suspicious activity monitoring
