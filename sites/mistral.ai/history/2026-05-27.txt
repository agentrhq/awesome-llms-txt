# Mistral AI
> Frontier AI for enterprises. Customizable, secure, and scalable solutions—from models to agents—built for performance, privacy, and control. Deploy on-prem, in the cloud, or at the edge with open-weight and commercial options.

Mistral AI empowers organizations with state-of-the-art (SOTA) models, AI assistants (Le Chat), and production platforms (AI Studio) to automate workflows, enhance decision-making, and drive innovation. Core offerings include:
- **Models**: Open-weight and commercial models (e.g., Mistral Large, Codestral) for coding, multilingual tasks, and edge deployment.
- **AI Studio**: End-to-end platform for building, deploying, and managing AI agents with observability and governance.
- **Le Chat**: Customizable AI assistant for enterprise knowledge, multimodal tasks, and secure collaboration.
- **Applied AI Services**: Expert-led custom training, fine-tuning, and deployment for domain-specific needs.
- **Partners**: Ecosystem of cloud providers, technology integrators, and consulting firms for seamless adoption.

All solutions prioritize data ownership, compliance, and scalability, with flexible pricing tiers (Free to Enterprise) and transparent SLAs.
