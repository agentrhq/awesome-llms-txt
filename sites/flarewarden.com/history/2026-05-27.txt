# FlareWarden

> FlareWarden is an uptime monitoring service for agencies, freelancers, and small-to-medium businesses.
> It monitors websites, APIs, SSL certificates, DNS records, and third-party dependencies
> with cross-region validation from multiple global locations. Checks run as frequently as
> every 30 seconds. Pricing from $0/month (free tier) to $229/month.
> https://flarewarden.com

## Documentation

- [Getting Started](https://flarewarden.com/docs/getting-started): Account setup and Smart Setup auto-configuration
- [Uptime Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/uptime): HTTP/HTTPS endpoint monitoring with response time tracking
- [Content Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/content): Page content change detection with 7 check types including regex, visual picker, and numeric range
- [Dependency Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/dependencies): Third-party service monitoring with 40+ integrations
- [SSL Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/ssl): Certificate expiration tracking with alerts at 7, 14, and 30 days before expiry
- [DNS Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/dns): DNS record change detection for A, MX, CNAME, and TXT records
- [Cron Monitors](https://flarewarden.com/docs/monitors/cron): Push-based monitoring for scheduled jobs, cron tasks, and background workers with start/complete/fail ping signals
- [Smart Setup](https://flarewarden.com/docs/smart-setup): Auto-configure all monitor types from a single URL
- [Alerts](https://flarewarden.com/docs/alerts): Email, SMS, and webhook notification configuration
- [Webhooks](https://flarewarden.com/docs/alerts/webhooks): Custom webhook integrations for alert delivery
- [Status Pages](https://flarewarden.com/docs/status-pages): Public branded status pages with subscriber email notifications
- [Status Page Branding](https://flarewarden.com/docs/status-pages/branding): Custom logos, colors, and domains for status pages
- [Status Page API](https://flarewarden.com/docs/status-pages/api): Programmatic access to status page data
- [Team Management](https://flarewarden.com/docs/team): Multi-user accounts with role-based access control
- [Team Roles](https://flarewarden.com/docs/team/roles): Role permissions and access levels
- [Incidents](https://flarewarden.com/docs/incidents): Automatic incident detection via cross-region validation
- [Maintenance](https://flarewarden.com/docs/maintenance): Scheduled maintenance windows to suppress false alerts
- [Data Export](https://flarewarden.com/docs/data-export): Export monitoring data
- [Settings](https://flarewarden.com/docs/settings): Account and application settings
- [Billing](https://flarewarden.com/docs/billing): Plan management and subscriptions

## Pricing

- [Pricing Page](https://flarewarden.com/pricing): Full plan comparison and signup

Free ($0): 15 monitors, 5 minutes checks, 2 team members, 1 status pages, 2 webhooks
Starter ($10/mo): 30 monitors, 1 minute checks, 5 team members, 2 status pages, 10 webhooks
Business ($29/mo): 100 monitors, 30 seconds checks, 25 team members, 15 status pages, 25 webhooks, custom domains
Growth ($79/mo): 300 monitors, 30 seconds checks, 50 team members, 15 status pages, 50 webhooks, custom domains
Agency ($229/mo): 1000 monitors, 30 seconds checks, unlimited team members, unlimited status pages, unlimited webhooks, custom domains

All plans include a yearly billing option (2 months free).
