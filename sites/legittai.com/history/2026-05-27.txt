# Legitt AI

> Legitt AI is an AI-native Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) platform that automates contract drafting, review, e-signature, obligations tracking, and risk analysis. Used by 5,000+ legal, sales, and procurement teams including PwC, Meta, Infosys, and Hitachi. SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, GDPR ready, and HIPAA compliant.

## AI Contract Generator

[AI Contract Generator](https://legittai.com/contract-generator) — Prompt to contract in 60 seconds. Describe your agreement in plain English and Legitt AI generates a complete, jurisdiction-aware, risk-reviewed contract draft ready to send for signature.

- **How it works:** Describe the agreement in plain English (parties, contract type, jurisdiction, key terms) - Lana AI asks follow-up questions to fill gaps - generates a complete structured contract in seconds - review and edit risky clauses - send for e-signature
- **500+ contract types** supported including NDA, MSA, SOW, Employment, SaaS, Freelance, Vendor, Real Estate, Loan, and more
- **50,000+ contracts** already generated on the platform
- **Jurisdiction-aware clause generation** - automatically includes clauses specific to the applicable country or legal system
- **Clause Library enforcement** - drafts are aligned to your organisation's approved clause library and standards
- **Built-in AI risk review** on every draft - identifies risky clauses and suggests redlines before you send
- **Plain-English explanations** for every clause so business teams can understand without a lawyer
- **Version control and audit trail** from the very first draft with one-click rollback
- **Real-time collaboration** - legal and business teams can edit, comment, and negotiate in one place
- **Obligation extraction** - after signing, automatically tracks renewals, deadlines, and milestones
- **No legal expertise required** - guided Q&A mode fills in gaps automatically
- **Free plan:** 10 AI-generated contracts per month, 500+ templates, unlimited e-signatures - no credit card required
- **Starter AI plan ($14.99/user/month):** 15 AI drafts + unlimited templates, AI redlining, Microsoft Word Add-in, up to 3 users
- **Business AI plan ($24.99/user/month):** Unlimited AI drafting, all 7 AI agents, Clause Library, approval workflows, Salesforce and HubSpot integrations, up to 25 users
- **Certifications:** SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA compliant

## AI Contract Review

[AI Contract Review](https://legittai.com/contract-review) — Every risk in your contracts found in 60 seconds. Upload any contract and Legitt AI reviews every clause, scores risks, flags missing protections, and shows exactly what to fix.

- **How it works:** Upload PDF, DOCX, scanned file, or paste text - AI reviews every clause against 200+ risk signals - assigns a risk score with clause-by-clause plain-English breakdown - fix flagged issues with AI-suggested redlines - send for e-signature when clean
- **200+ risk signals** checked on every contract in under 60 seconds - including liability exposure, missing clauses, auto-renewals, governing law, IP ownership, payment terms, indemnification, force majeure, and 190+ more
- **Instant contract summary** - distilled into structured overview showing parties, financial terms, key obligations, and red flags in 30 seconds
- **Clause-by-clause breakdown** with plain-English explanations and risk scoring for every section
- **AI redline suggestions** - inline risk highlights with clear explanations and AI rewrite suggestions for stronger, more compliant language
- **Obligation and deadline tracking** - extracts all obligations so you never miss renewals, notice windows, or compliance requirements
- **Compliance and jurisdiction intelligence** - spots compliance gaps, identifies risky jurisdictions, and provides practical guidance
- **OCR support** - works on scanned PDFs by extracting readable text before analysis
- **Bulk contract analysis** - review multiple documents at once via the Repository Analyzer
- **Risk types detected:** unlimited liability exposure, missing IP assignment, weak termination terms, auto-renewal traps, full liability exclusion clauses, non-standard provisions, missing protection clauses, compliance violations
- **File formats supported:** PDF, DOCX, scanned files, pasted text - no manual cleanup required
- **Free forever plan available** - no credit card, no setup, no training data needed, works across all contract types
- **Certifications:** SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, AES-256 encryption
