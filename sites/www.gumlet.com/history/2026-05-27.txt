- Digital Rights Management (DRM): Widevine and FairPlay support to prevent unauthorized access and redistribution of videos.

- Signed URLs: Temporary access to videos with time-limited URLs to restrict unauthorized sharing.

### How to Host and Stream videos with Gumlet

1. Sign Up: Create a Gumlet account if you haven’t already.
2. Upload Your Videos: 
   - You can upload videos directly through the [Gumlet Dashboard](https://www.gumlet.com/login). Alternatively, integrate with your platform (e.g., Vimeo, Wistia) using the import options.
3. Video Protection: Set up multi-layered protection for your videos including DRM encryption, dynamic watermarking, and signed URLs by visiting the [Video Protection](https://www.gumlet.com/video-protection/) page.
4. Customize Your Video Player: 
   - Use the [Customizable Video Player](https://www.gumlet.com/player-customization/) to create an ad-free player that matches your brand and provides the best user experience.
5. Start Streaming: If you want to broadcast live events, enable live streaming via the [Live Streaming](https://www.gumlet.com/live-streaming/) feature.
6. Optimize Video Delivery: 
   - Use our [Video CMS](https://www.gumlet.com/video-cms/) to organize, manage, and serve videos with minimal effort and maximum performance.
7. Monitor Performance: Use detailed analytics in the dashboard to track video performance and engagement.

### How to Start with Image Optimization

1. Sign Up: Create a Gumlet account to get started.
2. Upload Your Images: 
   - Upload your images via the [Gumlet Dashboard](https://www.gumlet.com/login), or integrate directly into your CMS like WordPress or Shopify.
3. Select Optimization Settings: 
   - Choose the optimization settings that fit your needs, such as image quality, resolution, and format (e.g., JPEG, PNG, WebP).
4. Use Image Operations: 
   - Utilize our [Image Operations](https://www.gumlet.com/image-operations/) tools to resize, crop, or apply filters to your images automatically.
5. Enable Auto-Responsive Resize: 
   - Ensure your images are always correctly sized for any device using the [Auto-Responsive Resize](https://www.gumlet.com/responsive-resize/) feature.
6. Optimize Your Images: 
   - Visit the [Image Compression](https://www.gumlet.com/image-compression/) page to compress images for faster load times and better SEO.
7. Monitor Performance: Track image performance through our analytics to see improvements in load time and SEO ranking.
