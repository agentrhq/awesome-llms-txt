# Liv Hospital

> Liv Hospital (Leading International Vision) is a JCI-accredited multi-specialty hospital group headquartered in Istanbul, Turkey. Operating 9 hospitals across Turkey, Azerbaijan, and Hungary, Liv Hospital offers advanced medical care in oncology, robotic surgery, stem cell therapy, IVF, orthopedics, neurosurgery, and 25+ specialties — serving international patients with 24/7 multilingual support.

## Hospitals

- [Liv Hospital Ulus](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-hospital-ulus/): Flagship campus in Ulus, Istanbul — opened 2013, JCI-accredited, Turquality brand, Center of Excellence
- [Liv Hospital Vadistanbul](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-hospital-vadistanbul/): Modern facility in Vadistanbul Park, Istanbul — multidisciplinary care with advanced technology
- [ISU Liv Hospital Topkapi](https://int.livhospital.com/isu-liv-hospital-topkapi/): University-affiliated hospital in Zeytinburnu, Istanbul — 300 beds, 11 operating rooms, 50,000 m²
- [ISU Liv Hospital Bahcesehir](https://int.livhospital.com/isu-liv-hospital-bahcesehir/): Istanbul Science University collaboration in Bahcesehir — established December 2016
- [Liv Hospital Ankara](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-hospital-ankara/): Central Turkey campus in Kavaklidere, Ankara — 27,000 m² facility
- [Liv Hospital Gaziantep](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-hospital-gaziantep/): Southeast Turkey campus — 40,000 m² facility with comprehensive specialties
- [Liv Hospital Samsun](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-hospital-samsun/): Black Sea region campus — 75 beds, 25 outpatient clinics, 4 operating rooms
- [Liv Bona Dea Hospital Baku](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-bona-dea-hospital-baku/): International campus in Baku, Azerbaijan — major healthcare investment in the Caucasus region
- [Liv Duna Medical Center](https://int.livhospital.com/liv-duna-medical-center/): European campus in Budapest, Hungary — Lechner Odon fasor 5

## Medical Departments

- [Oncology & Cancer Center](https://int.livhospital.com/cancer/): Precision oncology with MR-Linac, immunotherapy, genetic tumor profiling, and AI-supported treatment
- [Robotic Surgery Center](https://int.livhospital.com/robotic-surgery/): Da Vinci robotic systems for cardiac, urologic, thoracic, orthopedic, pediatric, and oncologic surgery
- [Stem Cell & Regenerative Medicine](https://int.livhospital.com/stem-cell/): Turkey's first hospital-based GMP-certified stem cell production center — treating vascular, neurological, and orthopedic conditions
- [IVF & Fertility Center](https://int.livhospital.com/ivf/): Assisted reproduction, egg freezing, genetic testing, and fertility preservation. Note: Turkish law requires legal marriage and own gametes — donor eggs/sperm, surrogacy prohibited
- [Cardiology](https://int.livhospital.com/cardiology/): Interventional cardiology, cardiac rehabilitation, congenital heart disease, arrhythmia management
- [Orthopedics & Spine Surgery](https://int.livhospital.com/orthopedics/): Joint replacement, sports medicine, spine surgery, pediatric orthopedics, robotic-assisted procedures
- [Neurology](https://int.livhospital.com/neurology/): Stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, movement disorders, and vascular neurology
- [Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery](https://int.livhospital.com/plastic-surgery/): Aesthetic and reconstructive procedures including rhinoplasty, breast surgery, body contouring
- [Urology](https://int.livhospital.com/urology/): Robotic prostatectomy, kidney stone treatment, andrology, urodynamic testing
- [Gynecology & Obstetrics](https://int.livhospital.com/gynecology/): Minimally invasive gynecologic surgery, high-risk pregnancy, menopause management
- [Gastroenterology](https://int.livhospital.com/gastroenterology/): Endoscopy, bariatric surgery, inflammatory bowel disease, hepatology
- [Ear, Nose & Throat (ENT)](https://int.livhospital.com/ear-nose-throat/): Head and neck surgery, hearing disorders, sinus treatment, sleep apnea
