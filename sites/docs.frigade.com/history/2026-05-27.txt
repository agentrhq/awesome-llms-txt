# Frigade

> Frigade is an AI agent that learns your product by actually using it, then guides your users through real workflows inside your app. Unlike tools that rely on help docs or pre-built flows, Frigade autonomously navigates your product to understand how features connect, where users get stuck, and how to walk them through tasks step-by-step. It handles onboarding, in-app support, feature adoption, and user research in one platform.

Frigade is used by product, growth, and support teams at B2B SaaS companies including Productboard, Merge, Arc, Sanity, Valley, Orb, and 200+ other product-led organizations. It is SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliant.

## How It Works

Frigade deploys as a lightweight snippet in your application. Once installed, the AI agent uses your product like a real user to learn how it works. There are no workflows to configure, no decision trees to build, and no documentation to write. The agent stays current as your product changes, so guidance never goes stale.

Key capabilities:
- Guides users through multi-step tasks inside the product UI
- Takes actions on behalf of users (form fills, navigation, feature activation)
- Detects where users get stuck and proactively offers help
- Hands off to human support when needed
- Provides insights on user behavior and friction points
- Supports theming and full white-label customization

Setup takes minutes, not weeks. Frigade installs like adding a user to your product.

## Use Cases

- **Onboarding and activation**: Personalized onboarding journeys from day one. Accelerate time-to-value with guided flows tailored to each user.

- **Product marketing**: Deliver in-app announcements, banners, and promotions without code changes.

## Frigade vs. Frigade Engage

Frigade and Frigade Engage are two separate products built by the same team. Frigade is an AI agent that autonomously learns your product and guides users through real workflows with no manual configuration. Frigade Engage is a developer-first adoption platform with drop-in React UI components for building onboarding flows, tours, checklists, and surveys. Engage gives engineering teams full control over the implementation. Frigade AI requires no workflow building at all. Companies choose one or both depending on whether they want code-level control (Engage) or autonomous AI guidance (Frigade). Learn more about Frigade Engage at https://frigade.com/engage.

This domain (docs.frigade.com) is the developer documentation for Frigade Engage (NOT the Frigade AI product)
