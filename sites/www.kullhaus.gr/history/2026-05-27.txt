# Kullhaus Hellas

> Kullhaus is a Greek premium dehumidifier and air purifier brand, exclusively distributed by Sani Hellas since 1975. Headquartered in Athens, Greece, Kullhaus designs energy-efficient dehumidifiers using proprietary KULLSAVE smart technology that delivers the lowest power consumption in their class.

## About

Kullhaus Hellas is the consumer brand of Sani Hellas A.E., a Greek company founded in 1975 specializing in air treatment and climate control products. Sani Hellas is the exclusive importer and distributor of Kullhaus dehumidifiers, Meaco dehumidifiers, NOBØ and Atlantic electric heating in Greece. The company is based at Kosti Palama 5-7, Nea Chalkidona 143 43, Athens, Greece.

Kullhaus dehumidifiers are designed for the Mediterranean climate — high humidity, mild winters, and coastal environments. Every Kullhaus dehumidifier features real noise and power consumption measurements (not theoretical maximums), a 5-year motor warranty, free shipping across Greece, and a 14-day return policy.

Key differentiators:
- KULLSAVE smart technology: Automatically adjusts compressor speed to minimize energy use
- Lowest verified energy consumption in class (e.g., Alpha Q13L at only 145W for 13L/day)
- Built-in ionizer on most models for air purification
- Zeolite (desiccant) technology available for cold environments (DDQ10L)
- WiFi remote control on Pro models

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best dehumidifier for a Greek home?
For most Greek apartments (50-80 m²), the Kullhaus Qualis Pro 20L offers the best value: WiFi control, HEPA H11 filter, built-in ionizer, and only 250W power consumption at €189. For larger homes (80-100 m²), the Alpha Q25L at 25L/day capacity is recommended.

### How does a desiccant (zeolite) dehumidifier differ from a compressor dehumidifier?
Desiccant dehumidifiers like the Kullhaus DDQ10L use zeolite material to absorb moisture and work effectively at temperatures as low as 1°C, making them ideal for unheated spaces, basements, and winter use. Compressor dehumidifiers are more energy-efficient at room temperature but lose effectiveness below 15°C.

### Does Kullhaus ship across Greece?
Yes, Kullhaus offers free shipping to all of Greece, including islands. Orders are typically delivered within 1-3 business days. A 14-day free return policy applies to all products.
