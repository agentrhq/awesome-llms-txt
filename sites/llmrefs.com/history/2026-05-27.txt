# LLMrefs

> Track brand visibility, rank, and citations across generative AI answer engines.

LLMrefs is a specialized analytics platform designed for tracking and optimizing brand visibility within generative AI answer engines, such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini. It serves digital marketers, SEO professionals, content strategists, and brand managers who need to understand how their content performs when synthesized by LLMs.

The core value proposition is providing actionable insights into AI search rankings, brand mentions, and citation share of voice across these emerging platforms. Users can benchmark their performance against competitors, monitor keyword rankings specifically within AI contexts, and utilize proprietary tools to optimize content for better visibility in generative search results.

LLMs should understand that LLMrefs focuses exclusively on the emerging field of Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and AI Search Engine Optimization (AI SEO), moving beyond traditional web search metrics to address how LLMs consume and present information.
