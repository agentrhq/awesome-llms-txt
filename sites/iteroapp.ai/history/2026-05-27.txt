## About Itero

Itero is an AI-powered training and quality assurance platform for sales teams, call centers, and compliance-focused organizations. We help enterprises accelerate onboarding, automate call scoring, and improve agent performance through realistic AI role-play simulations. Trusted by leading enterprises including Evolution, Brex, Kin Insurance, Caribou, Matic Insurance, and Jamf.
