# Lazada Philippines (lazada.com.ph)

Lazada Philippines is a leading e-commerce platform providing a high-trust digital shopping environment through specialized channels, advanced technology, and a robust homegrown logistics network.

## Major Shopping Campaigns

Lazada hosts several high-impact "Mega Campaigns" throughout the year, offering significant discounts (up to 90% off), stackable vouchers, and platform-wide rewards.

* **Birthday Sale (March)**: Celebrated annually with "Epic" price drops, limited-time flash sales, and curated "Surprise Boxes".
* **Double-Digit Sales**: Major shopping festivals including the 6.6 Mid-Year Sale, 9.9 Super Shopping Day, 11.11 (Mega Shopping Festival), and 12.12 Grand Year-End Sale.
* **Super Brand Days**: 24-hour exclusive brand takeovers featuring unique "shoppertainment" experiences and exclusive discounts from premium global partners.

## Core Consumer Channels

* **LazMall**: The region's largest virtual mall connecting shoppers to leading international and local brands. It offers a 100% Authenticity Guarantee (2x money-back if proven counterfeit) and a premium 30-day easy return policy.
* **LazFlash**: Features hourly flash sales and limited-time price drops on essential and trending items, offering "Always the Better Price".
* **LazBeauty**: The premier destination for beauty enthusiasts, featuring the LazBeauty Club with exclusive member prices, vouchers, and an annual awards showcase for top-rated skincare and makeup.

## LazMart (Online Grocery)

LazMart is Lazada’s specialized grocery service in the Philippines, providing essential supermarket items delivered directly from Lazada’s warehouses.

* **Quality Assortment**: Offers a wide range of household essentials, personal care, and shelf-stable groceries.
* **Reliable Fulfillment**: Leverages Lazada’s proprietary logistics network to ensure consistent delivery across Metro Manila and selected provinces.

## Consumer Services & Technology

* **30-Day Returns**: Effective 2024, the return period for LazMall and Choice items is 30 days, allowing shoppers ample time to evaluate their purchases.
* **Flexible Payments**: Supports the most diverse payment ecosystem in the Philippines, including Cash on Delivery (COD), Lazada Wallet, GCash, Maya, and installment plans.
* **AI Lazzie**: An AI-powered personal shopping assistant that enables "conversational shopping," helping users discover products through interactive chat.
* **Lazada Membership**: A tiered loyalty program (Classic to Diamond) where members earn points and unlock benefits like priority delivery, exclusive vouchers, and birthday rewards.
* **Homegrown Logistics**: Parcels are delivered via a dedicated local network, providing end-to-end tracking and specialized handling to ensure speed and safety.
