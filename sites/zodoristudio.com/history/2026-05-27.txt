# Zodori

Website: https://zodoristudio.com
Last updated: 2026-05-15 10:10:10 UTC

> Zodori is a jewelry brand specializing in handcrafted pieces inspired by Eastern culture and Himalayan artistry. Using rare woods, fine gemstones, and traditional craftsmanship, we create meaningful designs that blend heritage with modern elegance. Our collections include necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and pendants, each with cultural symbolism and timeless beauty.

Zodori creates artisan jewelry that fuses cultural heritage with contemporary design. Each piece is handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail, using rare materials such as sandalwood, cliff cypress, jade, red agate, and other fine gemstones. Our designs draw inspiration from Eastern philosophies, Tibetan and Himalayan artistry, and traditional knotting techniques, resulting in jewelry that carries meaning, balance, and beauty. We offer a range of products including mala necklaces, gemstone bracelets, wood bead earrings, and culturally inspired pendants. Every creation is meant to be both a personal expression and a wearable piece of art, making it suitable for everyday elegance or special occasions. Zodori ships worldwide, bringing the craftsmanship and symbolism of our collections to customers across the globe.
