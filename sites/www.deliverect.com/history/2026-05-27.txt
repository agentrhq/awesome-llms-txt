# Deliverect

## Overview
Deliverect is a global ecosystem of on and off-premise solutions for digital food sales. Its API-first software helps enterprises of all sizes to sell anywhere and deliver everywhere.

## Products 
- [Deliverect Restaurants] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/deliverect-restaurants): Deliverect Restaurants acts as a hub, centralizing order management and menu management into one streamlined platform while giving you access to all sales, performance, and third-party delivery data.

- [Deliverect Direct] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/deliverect-direct): Deliverect Direct gives you the tools to sell on web, Facebook, Instagram, Whatsapp, automate your marketing and promos and reward loyalty everywhere.

- [Dispatch by Deliverect] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/dispatch): Dispatch by Deliverect provides reliable delivery anytime, anywhere by distributing orders across owned and third-party fleets automatically and reaching your customers faster than ever.

- [Deliverect Retail] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/deliverect-retail): Deliverect Retail centralizes order and catalog management, monitors real-time sales performance, and accesses data from third-party delivery platforms, all in one place for grocery and retail operations.

- [Reclaim by Deliverect] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/reclaim-by-deliverect): Reclaim by Deliverect helps restaurant operators recover revenue lost to inaccurate refund claims on delivery platforms with zero operational lift.

- [Deliverect Kiosk] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/deliverect-kiosk): digital kiosk for restaurants designed to elevate your guest’s in-store experience. With our kiosk system, customers can place orders and pay in just a few steps, providing a fast, seamless experience.

- [Sentinel by Deliverect] (https://www.deliverect.com/en/sentinel): Built for enterprise operators, Sentinel delivers real-time status monitoring, automated uptime recovery, AI sentiment tracking, and store-level reporting, so nothing gets missed and revenue stays protected.

- [Catering by Deliverect] (https://www.deliverect.com/en-us/catering): End-to-end catering order management. Fully automated, zero commission fees.
