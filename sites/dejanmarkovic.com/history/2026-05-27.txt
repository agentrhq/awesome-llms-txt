# Dejan Markovic - WordPress Consultant Toronto

> Dejan Markovic is a Senior WordPress Developer, Solution Architect, and Team Lead based in Toronto, Canada. With 20+ years of web development experience, he has delivered enterprise WordPress projects for Rogers Media (Sportsnet, Chatelaine), Great-West Life, WP Engine, Munich Re, and the Ontario Ministry of Education. WordCamp Toronto lead organizer 2016, co-organizer 2014-2015, WPToronto Meetup co-organizer 2014-2016.

## Key Facts
- Location: Toronto, Canada
- Experience: 20+ years web development, 10+ years WordPress
- Specialization: Enterprise WordPress consulting, custom plugin and theme development, WooCommerce, REST API integrations, performance optimization
- Enterprise clients: Rogers Media (Sportsnet, Chatelaine), Great-West Life (Canada Life, London Life), WP Engine, Munich Re, Ontario Ministry of Education
- Community: Lead organizer WordCamp Toronto 2016; Co-organizer 2015, 2014; WPToronto Meetup co-organizer 2014-2016; speaker at WordCamps across North America and Europe
- Author: WordPress Responsive Theme Design Essentials (Packt Publishing); Technical Reviewer: Learning Yeoman
- Open source: HYPESocial Buffer Pro, Buffer My Post, Tweet Old Custom Post, Topcat Lite theme; WordPress core translation contributor
- Also runs: HYPESTUDIO (AI automation agency) - https://hypestudio.org
