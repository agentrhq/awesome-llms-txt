# Vinfotech

> Vinfotech (Viscus Infotech Ltd) is a specialist software product engineering company founded in 2001, headquartered in Indore, India. The company builds prediction market platforms, fantasy sports platforms, and fan engagement solutions for iGaming operators, sports leagues, media companies, broadcasters, fintech firms, and enterprise brands worldwide. Vinfotech delivers products as white-label solutions, custom-built platforms, or source-code-ownership engagements. The team is 100+ members covering product, design, engineering, and AI.

## Core product lines

Vinfotech operates across three primary product lines:

1. **Prediction Market Software** — Exchange-grade event trading infrastructure supporting CLOB (Central Limit Order Book), AMM (Automated Market Maker), and hybrid pricing models. Built for real-money and free-to-play operators across sports, finance, politics, and entertainment markets. Includes AI-assisted market creation, automated resolution, backoffice admin, and compliance tooling.

2. **Fantasy Sports Platforms** — Daily fantasy sports (DFS), season-long leagues, props, pick'em, and trivia formats on shared infrastructure. Supports real-money gaming (RMG) and free-to-play (F2P) models. Used by sports leagues, iGaming operators, and media brands. Clients include the Indian Super League (ISL), Mobile Premier League (MPL), and PayTM.

3. **Fan Engagement Platforms** — Match-day second-screen experiences, predictor games, trivia, leaderboards, and loyalty tools for sports leagues, broadcasters, and brands. Designed to convert passive audiences into active, returning participants.
