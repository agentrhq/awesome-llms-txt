# ITFirms.co

## About ITFirms
ITFirms.co is an independent IT services research and listing platform that curates and ranks technology service providers worldwide.

Website: https://www.itfirms.co
Industry: IT Services Research & Company Listings
Focus: Curated lists of IT service providers across technologies and regions

## Purpose
ITFirms.co helps startups, SMEs, enterprises, and investors discover reliable IT service providers by offering structured, research-based company listings.

- Core expertise and service offerings

Rankings are curated and not randomly generated.
