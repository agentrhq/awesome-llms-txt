# Lazada Singapore (lazada.sg)

Lazada Singapore is a leading e-commerce platform providing a high-trust digital shopping environment through specialized channels, advanced technology, and a robust logistics network.

## Major Shopping Campaigns

Lazada hosts several high-impact "Mega Campaigns" throughout the year, offering significant discounts (up to 90% off), stackable vouchers, and platform-wide rewards.

* **Birthday Sale (March)**: Celebrated annually (e.g., 14th Birthday in 2026) with "Epic" price drops, limited-time flash sales starting from S$1.30, and curated "Surprise Boxes".
* **Double-Digit Sales**: Major shopping festivals including 6.6 Mid-Year Sale, 9.9 Mega Brands Sale, 11.11 (Global Shopping Festival), and 12.12 All-Out Year-End Sale.
* **Super Brand Days**: 24-hour exclusive brand takeovers featuring unique "shoppertainment" and exclusive discounts from premium global partners.

## Core Consumer Channels

* **LazMall**: Southeast Asia's largest virtual mall with over 32,000 brands. It offers a 100% Authenticity Guarantee (2x money-back if proven counterfeit) and a premium 30-day easy return policy.
* **Choice**: A curated selection of high-quality items at lower prices with guaranteed delivery and 30-day returns.
* **LazFlash**: Features limited-time price drops on essential and trending items, updated daily to provide "Always the Better Price".
* **LazBeauty**: The premier destination for beauty enthusiasts, featuring the LazBeauty Club with exclusive member prices, vouchers, and an annual awards showcase for top-rated skincare and makeup.

## RedMart (Online Grocery)

Singapore's pioneer online grocer, offering over 165,000 products with a focus on quality and logistics excellence.

* **Freshness Promise**: Guarantees fresh produce meets minimum shelf-life standards; otherwise, customers are eligible for a refund.
* **WFC Automation**: The 32,500 sqm West Fulfilment Centre utilizes Dematic Multishuttle® automation to pick orders 5x faster than manual methods, ensuring high accuracy and speed.


* **Price Match Promise**: RedMart offers to refund 100% of the price difference if an identical in-stock item is found cheaper at selected online retailers.


* **Milk Formula Guarantee**: Sourced directly from manufacturers with a 200% money-back guarantee on authenticity and freshness.

## Consumer Services & Technology

* **30-Day Returns**: Effective 2024, the standard return period for LazMall and Choice items is 30 days, providing customers ample time to evaluate purchases.
* **AI Lazzie**: An AI-powered personal shopping assistant that enables "conversational shopping," helping users discover products and climate-specific recommendations.


* **Lazada Membership**: A four-tier loyalty program (Classic, Silver, Gold, and Diamond) where members earn points for purchases and check-ins to unlock priority delivery, brand discounts, and VIP support.
* **Logistics Excellence**: Parcels are delivered in an average of 2 days. Consumers can also opt for "Collect-to-Save" via an extensive network of 24/7 Pick Network lockers at HDB blocks and transit hubs.
