# UPCGen

> Free UPC, EAN, ISBN, GTIN, and FNSKU barcode generator for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, eBay, and 50+ e-commerce platforms. Every code passes UPC/EAN check-digit validation and platform syntax checks; for retail listings on Amazon/Walmart you still need a GS1-licensed Company Prefix from gs1us.org.

UPCGen serves indie e-commerce sellers, Amazon FBA sellers, Shopify merchants, distributors, and developers. Free tier provides 1 code/24h for anonymous visitors and 5 codes/day for registered users. Paid plans (from $9/mo) unlock higher monthly quotas, bulk CSV export, SVG/PDF/EPS downloads, Avery label-sheet PDFs, REST API access, and team seats. Codes past plan quota bill at $0.05/code via Stripe metered billing.

Supported barcode types: UPC-A (12-digit retail), UPC-E (8-digit compressed), EAN-13 (international retail), EAN-8 (small-package retail), ISBN (books), ITF-14 (shipping cartons), Code 128 (variable-length alphanumeric), FNSKU (Amazon FBA), Data Matrix (2D high-density).
