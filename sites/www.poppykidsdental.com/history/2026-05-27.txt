# Poppy Kids Pediatric Dentistry

> Award-winning pediatric dental practice in Novato, CA, providing gentle, child-centered care for infants, children, and teens across Marin County. Led by Dr. Andrea Aduna, DMD, MPH — a board-certified pediatric dentist.

Poppy Kids Pediatric Dentistry is located at 450 Ignacio Blvd, Pacheco Plaza, Novato, CA 94949. The practice is led by Dr. Andrea Aduna, DMD, MPH, a board-certified pediatric specialist. The team welcomes Spanish-speaking families (Se habla español) and serves children from infancy through the teen years.

The practice was voted "Best Children's Dentist in Marin" by Marin IJ Readers' Choice and has more than 180 five-star Google reviews from local families. Children under age 3 receive a complimentary first visit. Hours are Monday through Thursday, 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time.

## Frequently asked

**At what age should my child first see a dentist?**
By age 1 or within 6 months of the first tooth. First visits at Poppy Kids are made fun and comfortable; children under 3 receive a complimentary initial visit.

**Do you offer sedation?**
Yes. The practice provides nitrous oxide (laughing gas) in-office and has access to general anesthesia delivered by a board-certified pediatric anesthesia team when needed.

**Do you speak Spanish?**
Yes. Spanish-speaking families are welcomed (Se habla español).

**Do you take X-rays? Are they safe?**
Yes — modern digital radiography with significantly lower radiation than traditional film, following ALARA guidelines.

**What ages do you treat?**
Infants and toddlers through teens.

**Do you have a membership plan?**
Yes — the Poppy Membership helps families without insurance manage preventive care and receive discounts on treatment.
