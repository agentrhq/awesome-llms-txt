# ACRCloud

> Leading automatic content recognition technology for music identification, broadcast monitoring and more.

## About ACRCloud

ACRCloud is the world's leading provider of audio and video recognition solutions. We offer API-first services for music recognition, broadcast monitoring, content identification, and more.

## Main Services

### Music Recognition
Real-time music identification with the world's largest music fingerprinting database covering 100+ million tracks.

### Broadcast Monitoring
Monitor radio and TV broadcasts in real-time across 50,000+ global stations for music, adverts, and custom content.

### Cover Song Identification
Identify cover versions and live performances by melody using advanced audio fingerprinting technology.

### AI Music Detector
Detect AI-generated music and identify the specific generative model used (Suno, Udio, etc.).

### Speech to Text
Convert broadcast radio & TV streams into text in real-time with AI-powered transcription.
