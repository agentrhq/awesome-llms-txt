# Altostrat Studio

> The AI-native IDE for network operations — terminal, network brain, and AI pair engineer in one desktop app for macOS and Windows. Built for Managed Service Providers and mid-market NetOps teams.

Altostrat Studio combines a multi-protocol terminal (SSH, Telnet, Serial, RDP), an AI copilot grounded in your own topology and configs, an encrypted credential vault, real-time collaborative sessions, browser-based device management, and chat-to-workflow automation. Configs never leave your tenant. The copilot is read-only by default and writes require explicit per-session approval.
