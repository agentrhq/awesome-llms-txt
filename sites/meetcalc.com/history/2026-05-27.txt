# MeetCalc

> MeetCalc is a real-time meeting cost calculator designed to make the abstract cost of meetings tangible. It helps teams visualize resource allocation and drive a cultural shift towards better productivity.

## Core Tool
- [Meeting Calculator](https://meetcalc.com/): The main application interface for tracking meeting costs in real-time.
