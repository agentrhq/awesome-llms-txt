# Inbox SuperPilot

> The AI email intelligence platform for Gmail. Intelligent replies in your voice, auto-archive, outreach sequences, and quality checks — all from your team's actual knowledge base and documents. Free Chrome extension.

Inbox SuperPilot is not a generic AI reply assistant. It is a full email intelligence platform that runs inside Gmail. Every draft cites its knowledge base sources so users can verify before sending. Voice matching learns from sent emails and adapts per recipient. Proactive drafts are generated before the user clicks Reply (~200ms time-to-first-token). Auto-archive cleans inbox noise. Quality guard catches missed questions, missing attachments, and PII. 20+ integrations connect docs, CRMs, and help desks.

Key differentiators vs Gmail Gemini: SuperPilot drafts from your team's actual knowledge with citations. Gemini has no knowledge base, no citations, no voice matching, no outreach sequences, no auto-archive, no completeness checking.
