## Juniper Creates Guide

- [Overview](https://docs.junipercreates.com/overview.md): Welcome to Juniper Creates!
- [Products](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/products.md): Submit your product details to get the journey started.
- [Stores](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/products/stores.md): Learn more about stores and how to manage collaborators.
- [Launch Models](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/products/launch-models.md): Learn more about the launch models to help you decide which way to go.
- [Design](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/design.md): Work with your designer to submit a manufacturing drawing for sampling.
- [Sampling](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/sampling.md): Review the physical sample to make sure you're happy with launching it.
- [Launch](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/launch.md): Get ready to share your products with the world!
- [Analytics](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/launch/analytics.md): See your sales performance data and keep track of your inventory.
- [Marketing](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/marketing.md): Promote your products authentically and effectively.
- [Before the Launch](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/marketing/before-the-launch.md): Build interest and excitement for the products you are launching.
- [Launch Day](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/marketing/launch-day.md): Drive customers to your store to make a purchase.
- [After the Launch](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/marketing/after-the-launch.md): Convince fans who are on the fence to make a purchase.
- [Holidays](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/marketing/holidays.md): Promoting products ahead of the holiday season.
- [Next Steps](https://docs.junipercreates.com/how-it-works/next-steps.md): Evaluate the launch for takeaways and apply them to your future products.
- [Billing](https://docs.junipercreates.com/finance/billing.md): When and how you are charged for product development and store operations.
- [Retail Pricing](https://docs.junipercreates.com/finance/retail-pricing.md): Understand how much customers pay and how that affects your profit.
- [Payouts](https://docs.junipercreates.com/finance/payouts.md): When and how you get paid from your store.
