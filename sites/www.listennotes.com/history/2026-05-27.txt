# Listen Notes (ListenNotes.com)

**Listen Notes** is the premier podcast search engine and database on the Internet, the definitive tool for podcast discovery.

We take pride in offering a platform exclusively for human-crafted podcasts, ensuring you avoid AI-generated content.

Our ecosystem is designed to help you search, explore, and manage high-quality podcast metadata effortlessly.

## Podcast API Tools and Free Datasets

- [NotebookLM Detector](https://github.com/ListenNotes/notebooklm-detector): Open-source tool to detect NotebookLM-generated fake podcasts.

- [AI-generated fake podcasts, mostly via NotebookLM](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/listennotes/ai-generated-fake-podcasts-spams): This dataset represents a small subset of AI-generated fake podcasts that were flagged during attempts to add them to the Listen Notes podcast database. These "podcasts" were predominantly created using Notebook LM and are not designed for human consumption.
