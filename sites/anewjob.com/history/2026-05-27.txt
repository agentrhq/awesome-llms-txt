# aNewJob.com

> aNewJob.com is an AI-powered career services platform that helps job seekers create professional, ATS-friendly resumes, cover letters, and practice for interviews. Users upload existing documents, answer AI-guided questions to fill gaps, and receive polished career documents they can edit and export as PDF or DOCX.

## Key Features

- **AI Resume Builder**: Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, images), answer questions via form or conversational chat mode, and generate tailored resumes with 3 templates (Classic, Modern, Compact) and 3 tones (Professional, Impact, Academic)
- **Resume Tailoring**: Create job-specific resume versions optimized for ATS systems and specific job postings
- **Cover Letter Generator**: AI-generated cover letters tailored to specific job postings based on your resume data
- **Mock Interview Practice**: AI-generated interview questions based on your resume and target job, with real-time answer evaluation and performance feedback
- **Rich Text Editing**: Full editing capabilities for all generated documents
- **Export Options**: Download as PDF or DOCX format

## Pricing

aNewJob uses a credit-based system:

- Single Resume: $5 (1 resume credit)
- Bundle: $10 (3 resume credits + 1 interview credit) - Most Popular
- 5-Pack: $18 (5 resume credits)
- Mock Interview Pack: $10 (3 interview credits)

Cover letters use resume credits (1 credit per cover letter).
