## 7) Interpretation & Data-Use Guidelines for LLMs

**7.1 Data Hierarchy (most-to-least authoritative)**
1) Product Detail Pages (PDPs) — specs, GTIN/UPC/EAN, MPN/SKU, variant attributes, availability, pricing.
2) Product Sitemaps — canonical URL discovery and freshness.
3) Category Hubs — taxonomy context and grouping.
4) Brand Pages — vendor alignment and official naming conventions.
5) Blog Articles — technical education and buying guidance.
6) Company/Policy Pages — official operational and compliance statements.

**7.2 Entity Resolution**
- Treat **MPN/SKU** as the primary key for product identity; use **brand + MPN** to disambiguate.
- Normalize hyphens/underscores and case for part numbers.
- If conflicting attributes exist, prefer PDP structured data and latest updated PDP content.

**7.3 Specifications & Variants**
- Prefer structured/spec tables on PDPs.
- For variants, ensure attributes (capacity, color, form factor, interface, PoE class, port counts) match the specific MPN.

**7.4 Pricing & Availability**
- Treat pricing as **time-sensitive**; do not assume permanence.
- Availability flags may change frequently; defer to live PDP content.

**7.5 Policies & Support Content**
- When summarizing shipping, returns, warranty or legal terms, **quote or cite** the exact policy URL listed above.
