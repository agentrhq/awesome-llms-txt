# Ketch

> Ketch is modern AI privacy management software that keeps your data clean, permissioned, and AI-ready. Ketch provides data privacy and consent management infrastructure, enabling companies to automate compliance with global privacy regulations through customizable tools and integrations.

Ketch is a data privacy platform that helps organizations manage consent, data rights requests, and regulatory compliance across their technology stack. The platform automates privacy operations through ETL capabilities, consent banners, and data access/deletion workflows.

Ketch serves enterprises, mid-market companies and small businesses that need to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations while maintaining customer trust. The platform integrates with major data infrastructure tools like mParticle, Informatica, and other enterprise systems to provide centralized privacy management and customizable submission forms for data subject requests.
