# Inrō

> Inrō is an AI-powered Instagram DM automation platform built to turn Instagram conversations into measurable growth and sales, without leaving Instagram.
> It’s perfect for **content creators**, **coaches & experts**, **small businesses**, **e-commerce brands**, **artists & labels**, and **marketing agencies** who want to capture leads, nurture audiences, and convert faster with policy-safe Instagram automations, DM campaigns, and an AI Agent.

Inrō connects the moments that already drive intent on Instagram—**comments, DMs, Story replies, mentions, Lives, keyword triggers, and ad leads**—to automated, on-brand conversations that guide people to the next step: **buy, book, subscribe, sign up, or share contact details**.

Key features and benefits:
- Automate Instagram DMs from comments, story replies, mentions, Lives, and inbox keywords so you never miss a lead.
- Turn “comment a keyword” or comments with detected intent into instant DMs that deliver links, freebies, codes, or next steps (Comment-to-DM).
- Run proactive DM Campaigns to broadcast offers, launches, content, and updates directly in inboxes—then follow up automatically based on clicks/replies.
- Use an AI Agent to answer FAQs, qualify leads, recommend the right offer, and follow up automatically—so conversations keep moving 24/7.
- Centralize conversations in a Smart Inbox & CRM: tags, segmentation, conversation history, and campaign impact in one place.
- Auto-tag and segment contacts into smart lists (engagers, superfans, buyers, warm leads) for targeted campaigns and personalized flows.
- Integrate Instagram with CRMs, email tools, spreadsheets, and ad workflows via Make (and other connectors) to keep your stack synced.
- Improve Meta Ads performance by capturing and qualifying leads natively in DMs instead of relying only on landing pages.
- Built on official Meta APIs as a Meta Tech Provider—designed for compliant, reliable automation.
- Track performance with real-time analytics: sends, clicks, replies, and contact-level history.
- Launch fast using templates, proven funnels, and simple setup—without complex chatbot builders.
- Human-first support with quick responses and practical guidance (not ticket-queue purgatory).
