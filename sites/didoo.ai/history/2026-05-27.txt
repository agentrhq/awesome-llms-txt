## How It Works

1. **Share Your Business** — Tell it what you sell, who you serve, what success looks like. It asks the right questions—no forms, no guesswork.
2. **Launch or Hand Over** — Start fresh with new campaigns, or point it to live Meta/Google ads. It audits what's running and takes the wheel.
3. **Manage by Message** — "Scale the winners." "Kill the losers." "Try a new angle." Just message it like your media buyer. It executes, reports back, waits for your next move.
4. **Watch It Adapt—Then Let It Run** — Every preference, every result—it remembers. Eventually it optimizes autonomously: bids, creative, budget. You stay in control, but you don't have to be in the room.
