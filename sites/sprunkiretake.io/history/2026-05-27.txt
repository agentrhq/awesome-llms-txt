# Sprunki Retake: Play sprunki retake music game online

> `sprunkiretake.io` lets you play fan-made Sprunki music mixing game mod - Sprunki Retake. Combine sounds and characters to create unique beats, inspired by Incredibox.

## Key information:

- **Mix Music Creatively**: Use animated characters to mix beats, effects, melodies, and vocals.
- **Fan-Made Sprunki Mods**: Hosts multiple versions (like Retake) of the Sprunki project, inspired by Incredibox.
- **Audience**: Fans of Incredibox, Sprunki, music games, and creative web apps.

## Main Resources

- **[Main Site]**: `https://sprunkiretake.io/` - Website landing page.
- **[Game Hub]**: `https://sprunkiretake.io/all/` - Browse and select Sprunki mods here.

## Interaction Suggestions

- **Gameplay**: Users drag sound icons onto characters to create mixes.
