## 🔹 Competency Framework (LeaderApp360 & PES 360)
PES utilizes a research-backed competency model across 5 core dimensions with 20 competencies:

**1. Strategic Dimension (Estratégica)**
Competencies that generate and implement strategies facilitating organizational competitiveness and effectiveness.

- **Business Vision (Visión de negocio):** Ability to evaluate risks and opportunities to achieve desired objectives
- **Organizational Vision (Visión de la organización):** Ability to value and interact with different areas and departments beyond one's own activity
- **Customer Orientation (Orientación al cliente):** Ability to satisfy customer needs and respond to their requests and suggestions
- **Networking:** Ability to establish and maintain an extensive network of contacts with key people for the organization

**2. Interpersonal Dimension (Interpersonal)**
Competencies that facilitate personal relationships and the ability to motivate and develop collaborators.

- **Communication (Comunicación):** Ability to listen and transmit ideas effectively, using the appropriate channel at the right time
- **Delegation (Delegación):** Ability to provide collaborators with information and resources necessary for decision-making
- **Coaching:** Ability to help collaborators develop their potential
- **Teamwork (Trabajo en Equipo):** Ability to collaborate with team members to achieve a common goal

**3. Self-Management Dimension (Autogestión)**
Competencies oriented toward managing oneself with autonomy and proactivity, without need for external intervention or influence.

- **Time Management (Gestión del tiempo):** Ability to prioritize, schedule, and execute activities appropriately and within the expected timeframe
- **Stress Management (Gestión del Estrés):** Ability to remain calm in difficult or tense situations without losing self-control
- **Optimism (Optimismo):** Ability to value one's own capabilities, challenges, and difficulties from their most positive aspect
- **Initiative (Iniciativa):** Ability to contribute new ideas and undertake necessary changes creatively

**4. Self-Development Dimension (Autodesarrollo / Automotivación)**
Competencies oriented toward knowing one's own strengths and weaknesses, promoting learning and proactivity with an entrepreneurial spirit.

- **Learning (Aprendizaje):** Ability to acquire new knowledge, modify habits, and be open to change
- **Self-Awareness (Autoconocimiento):** Ability to know our strengths, weaknesses, and reactions with a proactive approach
- **Self-Criticism (Autocrítica):** Ability to accept and assume limitations, one's own errors, and feedback from others with the aim of improving
- **Ambition (Ambición):** Ability to establish challenges and follow them through to completion with an entrepreneurial spirit

**5. Self-Governance Dimension (Autogobierno / Autoliderazgo)**
Competencies oriented toward authentic leadership involving development of classical cardinal virtues: prudence (decision-making), justice (integrity), fortitude (self-control), and temperance (emotional balance).

- **Decision Making (Toma de Decisiones):** Ability to analyze and evaluate different alternatives and make appropriate decisions at the right time
- **Integrity (Integridad):** Ability to act with justice and rectitude in any situation
- **Self-Control (Autocontrol):** Ability to undertake costly actions, overcome difficulties, and complete tasks to the end
- **Emotional Balance (Equilibrio Emocional):** Ability to react with balance and proportion, maintaining a stable mood

**Note:** For CoachUp Skills (coaching assessment), dimensions 4 and 5 are labeled "Self-Motivation (Automotivación)" and "Self-Leadership (Autoliderazgo)" respectively, with identical competency definitions.

## 🔹 Motivational Profile Assessment
PES evaluates three motivational drivers that influence leadership style:

- **Integrator (Integrador):** Driven by sense of responsibility, considers people, and promotes organizational values
- **Executive (Ejecutivo):** Enjoys challenges and learning, generates new ideas
- **Strategist (Estratega):** Focused on strategic thinking and long-term organizational goals

Each assessed individual receives a percentage distribution across these three profiles showing their dominant motivational drivers.
