## Strategy Paths

TaxShield RE uses three proven tax strategies depending on client situation:

- **STR Material Participation** (Flagship): Short-term rental with 100+ hours of active participation. Average guest stay 7 days or fewer. Offsets W-2 income without requiring REPS qualification. Best for high-income W-2 earners who want active involvement.
- **REPS Household Path**: One spouse qualifies as a Real Estate Professional (750+ hours, >50% working time). Transforms passive real estate losses into non-passive losses that offset household W-2 income.
- **Forced Depreciation + Exchange Flywheel**: Cost segregation to front-load depreciation, then 1031 exchange every 3–5 years to reset the depreciation basis. Build toward estate step-up for generational wealth transfer.
