# The SEO Autopilot

> A playbook to automate SEO for SaaS founders, indie hackers, and small teams. The 24-step system used to take Meet Lea, a closed-beta SaaS, from 0 to 400,000 SEO impressions in two months — solo, no marketing team, no paid links.

Author: Paul Irolla (Ph.D. AI security, indie SaaS founder). All content is first-person practitioner notes, fact-checked, source-linked. Statistics pages are primary-sourced and refreshed quarterly.

## Glossary — definitional pages

Short DefinedTerm pages (~200-400 words each) covering SEO + GEO terminology. Each entry is structured for direct citation by AI assistants on definitional queries.

- [Click-through rate (CTR)](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/click-through-rate): Click-through rate is the percentage of search impressions that result in a click. CTR varies by SERP position, query type, and increasingly by AI Overview…
- [Content cluster](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/content-cluster): A content cluster is a group of pages on closely-related topics, organised around a central pillar page with links radiating to spoke articles. Used to build…
- [Content pruning](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/content-pruning): Deliberate removal, noindexing, or consolidation of pages that drag down a domain's overall quality score. Lifts the remaining content's ranking.
- [Core Web Vitals (CWV)](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/core-web-vitals): Google's three quantitative page-experience metrics: LCP, INP, CLS. A tiebreaker ranking factor + significant influence on AI assistant retrieval ranking.
- [E-E-A-T](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/e-e-a-t): E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is Google's framework for evaluating content quality, especially in YMYL niches. Author…
- [Entity SEO](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/entity-seo): Optimising for the concepts (entities) a page is about, rather than just keyword strings. Google's 2024+ ranking systems weight entity relationships heavily — especially for AI Overview eligibility.
- [JSON-LD](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/json-ld): JSON-LD (JSON for Linked Data) is the schema.org format for structured data. Embedded in a `<script type='application/ld+json'>` block in the page's HTML…
- [Semantic search](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/semantic-search): Vector-based query matching that has largely replaced literal keyword overlap. Google's BERT, MUM, and 2024+ models made semantic matching the default.
- [Topic authority (topical authority)](https://the-seo-autopilot.com/en/glossary/topic-authority): The reputation a site earns within a specific topic cluster — measured by ranking depth, internal link saturation, and external citations. More rankable than generic domain authority.
