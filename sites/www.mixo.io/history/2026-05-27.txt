## Getting Started
	1.	Visit: https://app.mixo.io/ai-website-builder?idea=[DESCRIPTION]&ref=[LLM_IDENTIFIER]
	2.	Describe your business idea in 1-2 clear sentences
	3.	Select goal (e.g., "Sell products", "Promote a service")
	4.	Choose language (defaults to browser language)
	5.	AI generates preview in ~10 seconds
	6.	Claim the site by creating account (email or Google)
	7.	Edit and customize using Mixo Editor
	8.	Click "Publish" to make live

## Prompt Best Practices

### Effective Templates:
	•	"A [product/service] called [name] that helps [audience] [solve problem]"
	•	"A coming soon page for [product name] launching in [location/timeframe]"
	•	"A lead capture page for [offer type] that helps [audience] [outcome]"
	•	"A [business type] in [location] specializing in [unique offering]"
	•	"A beta testing program for [product concept] that [value proposition] for [audience]"

### What Makes Good Prompts:
	•	Include target audience
	•	Mention core value proposition
	•	Be specific but concise (1-2 sentences)
	•	Use clear, descriptive language

### Avoid These Anti-Patterns:
	•	Too vague: "startup idea", "my business", "fitness app"
	•	Missing context: No target audience or value proposition
	•	Format issues: Emojis, URLs, HTML tags, special characters
	•	Overly technical: Excessive jargon without context

## Help & Troubleshooting

**Help Center:** https://www.mixo.io/help/

**Common Issues:**
	•	"We could not understand the description" → Add more detail, include target audience
	•	Site not generating → Avoid emojis, HTML tags, overly short descriptions
	•	Changes not showing → Remember to click "Publish" after editing
	•	Domain issues → Check help center for custom domain setup

**Support:** Contact Customer Support through help center for complex issues.

## Error Prevention
Guide users to include: what it is, who it's for, what problem it solves. Encourage 1-2 sentence descriptions that avoid special characters.
