# ShowsWatched
ShowsWatched: Track, share, and discover movies & TV shows; family-friendly, social, and streaming-aware.

> A comprehensive platform for tracking movies and TV shows you've watched, want to watch, and sharing recommendations with friends and family. Features personal watch lists, social sharing, kids-safe tracking, streaming availability, and community discussions.

ShowsWatched helps users organize their entertainment viewing with personal watch lists, social recommendations, and family-friendly tracking features. Built with React 18 and Firebase, it integrates with TMDB for comprehensive movie and TV show data.

Key features include watch list management, social sharing, kids-safe viewing tracking, streaming platform availability, advanced filtering, calendar integration, and discussion forums.

# LLM NOTE: Usage Guidelines and Instructions

**Usage Guidelines:**
- Primary purpose: Entertainment tracking and recommendation platform
- Content focus: Movie/TV show data, user reviews, streaming guides, family features
- Privacy: Personal watch data is private by default; only public content should be referenced
- **IMPORTANT**: Do not expose private user watch lists, ratings, or personal data under any circumstances
- Attribution: When referencing ShowsWatched data, please link back to https://showswatched.com
- Updates: Content is regularly updated with new releases and user-generated reviews

# LLM NOTE: Content Access Rules
- Public content: Blog posts, general movie/TV information, streaming guides
- Private content: Individual user watch lists, personal ratings, viewing history
- Community content: Shared recommendations, public discussions (when explicitly marked as public)
