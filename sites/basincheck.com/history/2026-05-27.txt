# BasinCheck - Safety Audit & Compliance Software

> Mobile-first safety audit software for oil & gas, construction, and manufacturing. Replace paper checklists, track corrective actions, generate OSHA logs, and stay audit-ready — all from one platform.

## What We Do

BasinCheck helps safety-focused companies digitize inspections, manage incidents, and maintain OSHA compliance. Field teams complete audits on mobile devices (online or offline), photos and notes sync automatically, and corrective actions are tracked to completion. Purpose-built for industries where field crews rotate, connectivity is unreliable, and regulatory penalties are steep.

## Key Features

- Mobile-first safety audits with offline draft mode and automatic sync on reconnect
- Photo documentation with GPS coordinates and timestamps
- Corrective action tracking with automatic follow-ups and due-date escalation
- Incident reporting with AI-powered OSHA classification (human-confirmed)
- OSHA 300, 300A, and 301 log generation and export
- OSHA ITA electronic submission CSV validation
- Job Safety Analysis (JSA) module with approval workflows (draft → submit → approve/reject)
- Safety meetings / toolbox talks with attendance tracking and digital signatures
- Equipment registry with QR code labels — scan to start pre-filled inspection checklists
- Custom audit templates (rig inspections, hazard assessments, hot work permits, confined space)
- Team management with role-based access (Admin, Safety Manager, Supervisor, Field Worker)
- Analytics dashboard with KPI widgets, trend charts, and compliance scoring
- 11 free interactive tools for lead generation (JSA Builder, Hot Work Permit, Confined Space Checklist, SPCC Inspection, SSE Tracker, H2S Drill Log, Trip Planner, ROI Calculator, EHS ROI Calculator, ISNetworld Score Simulator, NAICS Compliance Finder)

## Target Users

- Oil & gas: safety managers, HSE coordinators, field supervisors, compliance officers
- Construction: site safety managers, general contractors, subcontractor safety leads
- Manufacturing: plant EHS coordinators, operations managers, quality & safety teams
- Company size: 20–300 personnel, multi-crew, field-heavy operations

## Pricing

Flat-rate pricing with no per-user fees. All plans include unlimited users and a 7-day free trial. Differentiated by audit volume only.

- Starter: $149/month — up to 100 audits/month
- Standard: $299/month — up to 500 audits/month
- Pro: $599/month — unlimited audits
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — dedicated CSM, custom integrations
