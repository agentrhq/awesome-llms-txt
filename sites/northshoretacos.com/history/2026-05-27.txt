# North Shore Tacos

> Baja-style Mexican restaurant and food truck on Oahu's North Shore, Hawaii. Founded in 2010 by Joseph "Joey" Fullmer. Known for crispy fried fresh fish tacos (Surfin' Tacos), tropical drinks, and scratch-made food served daily at two locations: the Hauula restaurant and the Sharks Cove food truck.

## About
- Founded in 2010 by Joey Fullmer after years of Baja surf trips inspired him to bring fish tacos to Oahu's North Shore.
- Elen Corazzari joined in 2014 as co-owner and operations manager.
- The Sharks Cove food truck launched in 2015.
- Joey converted a humble building in Hauula into the flagship restaurant.
- Everything is made from scratch daily using locally sourced ingredients and fresh, never-frozen fish.
- The signature Surfin' Tacos feature crispy fried fish, jack cheese, fresh lettuce, pico de gallo, and Surf Sauce.
