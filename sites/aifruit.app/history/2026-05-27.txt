## About AI Fruit

AI Fruit is a vertical SaaS focused on the AI fruit video trend that exploded on TikTok in late 2025. Instead of asking users to engineer prompts for generic video models like Veo or Sora, AI Fruit ships pre-tuned templates and routes generation across 6 underlying models so creators get publishable 9:16 clips in roughly 60-90 seconds.

### Key Features

- **50+ Fruit Templates** organized across 8 content tags. Click and generate, no prompt engineering required.
- **6 AI Models** with different strengths — pick the right trade-off per clip:
  - **Wan 2.6** (Replicate) — Multi-shot narrative, embedded audio, up to 15s
  - **Sora 2** (OpenAI) — Cinematic motion, up to 12s
  - **Veo 3.1** (Google) — HD with synced audio, up to 8s
  - **Seedance 2.0** (ByteDance) — HD with audio, up to 15s
  - **Kling 3.0 Omni** (Kuaishou) — Cinematic look, up to 10s
  - **Grok Imagine** (xAI) — Fast generation, up to 15s
- **TikTok-Native Formats**: 9:16 vertical default, also supports 16:9, 1:1. No watermark on paid plans.
- **Fast Generation**: ~60-90 seconds with the default fast model (Grok Imagine). Premium models take 2-3 minutes for higher cinematic quality.
- **Commercial License**: All generated videos can be used commercially — TikTok monetization, brand content, ads. No attribution required.

### How It Works

1. **Pick a Template** — Browse 50+ templates filtered by 8 tags, or describe your own scene.
2. **Choose a Model** — One subscription covers all 6 models; switch per clip without separate accounts.
3. **Generate & Download** — Wait ~60-90 seconds, download MP4 in 9:16 (TikTok), 16:9 (YouTube), or 1:1 (Instagram).

## FAQ

### What is AI Fruit?
AI Fruit is a hosted SaaS that generates fruit-themed short videos for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts using 6 AI models and 50+ pre-built templates across 8 content tags.

### Who is AI Fruit for?
Solo creators and small social media teams publishing fruit-themed content (fruit ASMR, fruit drama, baby fruit characters, glass cutting) at volume. Best for creators posting 5-20 short videos per week who care about consistency over maximum creative control.

### How much does AI Fruit cost?
Two paid tiers: Creator at $15/month (600 credits, up to 1080p, 3 concurrent generations) and Pro at $30/month (1500 credits, 6 concurrent). Yearly billing is 33% off, bringing the monthly cost to $10 for Creator and $20 for Pro. One-off credit packs are also available.

### Is AI Fruit free to try?
Yes. New users get 20 free credits at sign-up plus 2 bonus credits per day for daily login. That's enough to test 3-5 generations on the default fast model before any payment is required.

### Which AI models does AI Fruit support?
Six models: Wan 2.6 (Replicate), Sora 2 (OpenAI), Veo 3.1 (Google), Seedance 2.0 (ByteDance), Kling 3.0 Omni (Kuaishou), and Grok Imagine (xAI). All six are available on every paid plan — one subscription, six models.

### Can I use AI Fruit videos commercially?
Yes. Videos generated on paid plans can be used commercially, including TikTok monetization, brand-sponsored content, and paid social ads. There's no watermark on paid output and no attribution required.

### Does AI Fruit add a watermark?
No watermark on paid plans. Creator and Pro tiers produce clean, unbranded MP4 files.

### What aspect ratios are supported?
9:16 vertical (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) is the default. Also supports 16:9 (landscape) and 1:1 (square), depending on the underlying model.

### How long does it take to generate a fruit video?
Most templates finish in roughly 60-90 seconds with the default fast model (Grok Imagine). Premium models like Kling 3.0 Omni and Seedance 2.0 take 2-3 minutes for higher cinematic quality.

### Does AI Fruit work with TikTok directly?
Output is delivered as 9:16 MP4 ready to upload to TikTok, Reels, or Shorts. No native auto-publish — download and upload through TikTok's app.

### Can I upload my own reference image?
Yes. Image-to-video is supported on most of the underlying models, useful when you want a specific fruit look or branded packaging shot to anchor the clip.

### How is AI Fruit different from generic AI video tools like Veo, Sora, or Kling?
AI Fruit is a vertical SaaS — it ships pre-tuned templates for the fruit-video niche and routes across 6 models internally. Generic tools require prompt engineering and access to one model per subscription. AI Fruit trades prompt freedom for higher hit-rate on fruit content and one-subscription model access.
