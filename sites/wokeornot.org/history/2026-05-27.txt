## Dynamic URL Pattern

Dynamic title pages use this pattern:

- `/{slug}`

Slug formatting rules (for guessing URLs):

- Lowercase the title.
- Remove punctuation/special characters.
- Replace spaces/underscores with hyphens.
- Collapse repeated separators into one hyphen.
- Trim leading/trailing hyphens.
- Release years are encouraged in slugs, especially to disambiguate different versions; append the year at the end (e.g. `-1989`, `-2023`).

Examples:

- `The Dark Knight` -> `/the-dark-knight`
- `Spider-Man: No Way Home` -> `/spider-man-no-way-home`
- `Dune (2021)` -> `/dune-2021`
- `The Little Mermaid` (different versions) -> `/the-little-mermaid-1989` or `/the-little-mermaid-2023`
