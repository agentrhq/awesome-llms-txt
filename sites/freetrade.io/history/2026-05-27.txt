## Account Types

* [Stocks & Shares ISA](https://freetrade.io/stocks-and-shares-isa): Tax-efficient investing for UK residents, included with all plans.
* [Junior ISA (JISA)](https://freetrade.io/junior-isa): A tax-efficient account for investing on behalf of a child, available to UK residents.
* [Self-Invested Personal Pension (SIPP)](https://freetrade.io/pension/sipp): Grow your pension investments with flexibility, included with all plans.
* [General Investment Account (GIA)](https://freetrade.io/general-investment-account): A flexible account for standard investing, included with all plans.
* [Compare All Plans](https://freetrade.io/compare-plans): Side-by-side comparison of account features, benefits and subscription fees.

> Note: Pricing, FX rates, and plan features may change.
> Always refer to [https://freetrade.io/compare-plans](https://freetrade.io/compare-plans) for the latest details.

## Investment Types

### Core Investments

* [Stocks and Shares](https://freetrade.io/how-to-invest-in-stocks): Direct investment in individual companies through the stock market.
* [ETFs](https://freetrade.io/etfs): Diversified baskets of assets traded on the stock exchange with low fees and flexibility.
* [Mutual Funds](https://freetrade.io/mutual-funds): Professionally managed pooled investments for long-term diversification.
* [Investment Trusts](https://freetrade.io/investment-trusts): Closed-ended funds investing in diversified portfolios, traded like stocks.
* [Ready-made Portfolios](https://freetrade.io/ready-made-portfolios): Pre-built, diversified portfolios for investors who prefer a hands-off approach.

### Income & Capital Preservation

* [Gilts](https://freetrade.io/gilts): UK government bonds offering stable returns and lower risk.
* [Treasury](https://freetrade.io/treasury): Short-term government-backed investments designed for capital preservation.

### Advanced & Higher-Risk

* [Fractional Shares](https://freetrade.io/fractional-shares): Invest in expensive stocks from as little as £2 by owning a fraction of a share.
* [REIT Stocks](https://freetrade.io/reit-stocks): Real estate investment trusts offering property market exposure and regular income.
* [SPACs](https://freetrade.io/spac): Public shell companies that merge with private firms to bring them to market.
* [Share Lending](https://freetrade.io/share-lending): Earn passive income by lending out your eligible shares to institutions.

## Scope Clarification

Freetrade does not offer:

* Financial advice
* CFDs or spread betting
* Direct cryptoasset trading (note: crypto ETNs are available as a high-risk instrument — see [Crypto ETN risks explained](https://freetrade.io/crypto-etns/risks-explained))
* Leveraged or day-trading products
