# Lanier Pristine

> Boutique cleaning by small, dedicated teams who know your home — not rotating strangers from a franchise

Lanier Pristine is a licensed, insured, and bonded residential and commercial cleaning company based in Gainesville, GA. Founder Mara Guilford has been cleaning North Georgia homes for 20+ years (since arriving from Curitiba, Brazil in 2005); she founded the business in 2014 as All Seasons Cleaning Services and rebranded to Lanier Pristine in 2022 when the operation refocused on the Lake Lanier corridor. 1,000+ homes cleaned, 5.0-star Google rating from 18+ reviews. Same small, dedicated team every visit — never rotating strangers from a franchise.
