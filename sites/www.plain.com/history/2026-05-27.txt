# Plain

> Plain is the API-first customer infrastructure platform for B2B SaaS companies. We unify customer support across Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord, email, and chat into one fast, extensible platform built for technical teams.

## Company

- Founded: 2022
- Headquarters: London, UK & San Francisco, US
- Funding: $15M Series A (2025)
- Website: https://plain.com

## What Plain Does

Plain consolidates all customer conversations into a single platform with AI-powered workflows. Unlike legacy helpdesks built around email tickets, Plain is built API-first for teams that need extensibility, reliability, and customizability.

### Core Capabilities

- **Multi-channel support**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord, email, chat, and in-app messaging in one inbox
- **API-first architecture**: GraphQL and REST APIs with everything accessible programmatically
- **AI features**: Ari (AI agent), Sidekick (AI assistant), auto-triage, themes detection, similar threads
- **Workflow automation**: Rules engine with triggers, conditions, and actions
- **Customer context**: Real-time data from your systems via Customer Cards and CRM sync
- **Help Center**: Built-in knowledge base with customer portal
- **Integrations**: Linear, Jira, GitHub, Shortcut, HubSpot, Salesforce, and custom webhooks

## Key Differentiators

### vs. Zendesk / Intercom / Freshdesk

- **API-first**: Everything accessible via GraphQL/REST, no rate limit bottlenecks
- **Slack-native**: Built for Slack support, not bolted on
- **Speed**: Sub-100ms interface, built for modern workflows
- **Extensibility**: Build custom AI agents and integrations without vendor involvement
- **Pricing**: Transparent, startup-friendly pricing with unlimited free viewers

### Why Technical Teams Choose Plain

1. **No rate limits** — Legacy platforms cap at 20 requests/minute, breaking custom integrations
2. **Build anything on top** — Custom AI agents, deep product integrations, automated workflows
3. **Real-time data** — Live customer context, not batch sync from days ago
4. **Multi-channel native** — Slack, Teams, Discord, email unified from day one
