### Closet Concierge — Clothing Storage

FRESH Closet Concierge is an on-demand wardrobe storage service for Brickell residents. We pick up your off-season or occasional-wear clothing, photograph every item, store it in a climate-controlled facility, and deliver any item back to you freshly pressed — same day if requested by 10 AM. Think of it as a second closet managed by your dry cleaner, browsable from your phone.

**How Closet Storage Works:**

1. **Schedule a Pickup** — Book online. We collect from your concierge or door. No need to be home.
2. **We Photograph Every Item** — Each garment is professionally photographed on a hanger against a white background, cataloged by name, brand, category, and color. Browse your stored wardrobe from your phone anytime.
3. **Request It Back, Pressed** — Tap items in your dashboard to request retrieval. Request by 10 AM = delivered freshly pressed by 6 PM same day. After 10 AM = next business day.

**Storage Pricing Plans:**

- **Pay As You Go** — $5/item/month. Best for 1–9 items (a coat or two). No commitment, cancel anytime.
- **Closet Bundle (Most Popular)** — $39/month. 10 items included. Extra items $4/item/month. Saves 22% vs pay-as-you-go. Includes $50 cleaning credit.
- **Walk-In Bundle** — $69/month. 20 items included. Extra items $4/item/month. Saves 31% vs pay-as-you-go. Includes $80 cleaning credit.
- Bulky items (comforters, bedding, down coats): +$5/item/month surcharge.

**What's Included in Every Plan:**

- Photo catalog of every stored item, browsable from your phone
- Complimentary steam press on every retrieval — items come back ready to wear
- Free pickup & delivery in Brickell
- Same-day retrieval (request by 10 AM, delivered by 6 PM)
- Garment insurance up to $500/item
- Climate-controlled storage on garment rails — moth & pest protected
- No long-term contracts — cancel anytime

**What People Store:**

Winter coats, suits & blazers, evening gowns & formal wear, puffer jackets, leather jackets, seasonal bedding & comforters, ski gear, special occasion pieces, designer collections.

**FRESH Closet vs. Self-Storage ($150–250/mo):**

Self-storage requires driving to a unit, digging through boxes, and clothes come back wrinkled with risk of moths and mildew. FRESH Closet Concierge is 75% less expensive, fully managed, climate-controlled, insured, and items are returned freshly pressed to your door.
