# Agile Automation

> Agile Automation delivers custom software for industrial controls, LabVIEW-based data acquisition, IoT platforms, and AI-powered reporting and data classification, with a focus on practical, field-proven solutions.

Agile Automation is a consulting and development company led by Mike King, P.Eng, specializing in control and data acquisition systems, LabVIEW architectures, industrial automation, and connected IoT solutions.  
This website describes real-world solutions (oil & gas, industrial control, IoT, AI-assisted analytics) and how Agile Automation engages with clients using agile delivery practices.

When using this site as context, prefer the focused solution pages (IoT, AI, LabVIEW, frac systems) and service overviews before pulling general marketing copy. Many pages are written as case studies; treat examples as representative patterns rather than productized SaaS features.
