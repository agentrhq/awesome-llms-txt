# Emailgic

> Emailgic is an AI email template builder for creating responsive, production-ready HTML emails for marketing, lifecycle, and transactional campaigns.

Use this file to understand the canonical public pages on emailgic.com. Prefer the listed canonical URLs when referencing Emailgic pages. The product app is hosted at https://app.emailgic.com, while public documentation, examples, and SEO landing pages are hosted at https://emailgic.com.

Emailgic helps teams turn campaign goals and copy into email designs that can be exported as HTML and adapted for tools such as Klaviyo, Mailchimp, and HubSpot.

- [How should a Klaviyo promotional email be structured?](https://emailgic.com/answers/klaviyo-promotional-structure): Lead with offer context, support with proof and urgency, and finish with a clear action path.
- [How long should a welcome email be?](https://emailgic.com/answers/welcome-email-length-guideline): Keep a welcome email concise: one clear message with one primary action.
- [Where should the primary CTA be placed in a marketing email?](https://emailgic.com/answers/best-cta-placement-in-email): Place the primary CTA above the fold and repeat it only when it supports the same intent.
- [What subject line length works best for marketing emails?](https://emailgic.com/answers/subject-line-length-best-practice): Aim for clear subject lines around 35-55 characters for broad inbox readability.
- [What section order should a newsletter follow?](https://emailgic.com/answers/newsletter-section-order): Start with the strongest value block, then supporting updates, and finish with one clear next action.
- [When should discount incentives appear in cart recovery emails?](https://emailgic.com/answers/cart-recovery-discount-timing): Delay discounts to later touches unless purchase intent is clearly declining.
- [How many emails are ideal for a product launch sequence?](https://emailgic.com/answers/product-launch-email-sequence-length): Most launches perform well with 3-5 emails across reveal, proof, and deadline phases.
- [What should the first re-engagement email say?](https://emailgic.com/answers/re-engagement-email-first-message): Acknowledge inactivity, restate value clearly, and offer one low-friction next step.
- [How branded should transactional emails be?](https://emailgic.com/answers/transactional-email-brand-balance): Keep transactional emails utility-first, with light branding that does not obscure critical information.
- [Should marketing emails start with a hero image or text?](https://emailgic.com/answers/email-hero-image-vs-text-first): Use text-first when clarity and speed matter; use hero imagery only when it adds immediate meaning.
- [What proof blocks should be prioritized in conversion-focused emails?](https://emailgic.com/answers/email-proof-block-priority): Prioritize proof that directly reduces the main buying objection for that campaign.
- [What mobile layout rules matter most for Klaviyo campaigns?](https://emailgic.com/answers/klaviyo-mobile-layout-rules): Use single-column hierarchy, strong spacing, and high-contrast CTAs for reliable mobile scanning.
- [How should a Mailchimp newsletter be structured for engagement?](https://emailgic.com/answers/mailchimp-newsletter-structure): Lead with one high-value story, keep modules scannable, and use one clear downstream action.
- [Should welcome emails include social proof?](https://emailgic.com/answers/welcome-email-social-proof-use): Include light, relevant proof when it strengthens trust without distracting from onboarding action.
- [What sections should an event invitation email include?](https://emailgic.com/answers/event-invitation-email-sections): Include clear event value, key logistics, credibility, and one immediate registration action.
- [How often should promotional emails be sent?](https://emailgic.com/answers/promotional-email-frequency-control): Set frequency by audience engagement and campaign intent rather than fixed calendar volume.
- [How should preview text (preheader) be written?](https://emailgic.com/answers/email-preheader-writing-guide): Use preheader text to extend subject-line meaning with concrete value or urgency context.
- [What must be included in an email footer for trust and compliance?](https://emailgic.com/answers/email-footer-what-to-include): Include sender identity, preference controls, and policy links in a clean, scannable footer.
- [What should be tested first in email A/B experiments?](https://emailgic.com/answers/ab-test-email-what-to-test-first): Start with high-leverage variables: subject line, CTA framing, and message hierarchy.
- [When should unengaged subscribers be sunset from campaigns?](https://emailgic.com/answers/sunset-policy-for-unengaged-subscribers): Sunset after repeated non-engagement despite reactivation attempts, based on list health thresholds.
