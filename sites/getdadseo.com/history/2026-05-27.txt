# DadSEO

> Strategic SEO intelligence platform that connects to Google Search Console and gives you a real diagnosis — not a score.

DadSEO is an AI-powered SEO analytics tool built for founders, marketers, and agency leads who want actionable strategy instead of meaningless checklists. Connect your Google Search Console (read-only), and DadSEO backfills up to 16 months of search data to run a strategic diagnosis using the Strategy Kernel framework (Threats, Gaps, Opportunities).

## Core Capabilities

- **Strategic SEO Audit**: AI-powered diagnosis using the Strategy Kernel framework (inspired by Richard Rumelt). Identifies threats, gaps, and opportunities ranked by impact — not a score out of 100.
- **Google Search Console Integration**: Read-only OAuth connection. Automatic backfill of up to 16 months of historical search performance data (queries, pages, clicks, impressions, CTR, position).
- **AI Assistant**: Chat interface that has full context of your site's audit results, GSC data, and competitor landscape. Ask anything and get concrete, data-backed answers.
- **20+ SEO Tools**: Site overview, query analysis, page performance, competitor analysis, content intelligence, technical audit, schema audit, sitemap audit, internal links, image optimization, hreflang, off-page analysis, AI visibility (GEO), growth tracking, topic research, programmatic SEO, and more.
- **Content Intelligence**: JTBD-aligned content analysis, query fan-out evaluation, and content gap identification.
- **Competitor Analysis**: See which competitors outrank you and for which queries.
- **AI Visibility (GEO)**: Generative Engine Optimization audit — checks your visibility across AI search surfaces and generates llms.txt files.
